# Kirby — Waste Eater (Mini Desk Bin)

**Source:** [Kirby Waste Eater by BODY3D on MakerWorld](https://makerworld.com/en/models/110045)

Kirby with a hollow body — his mouth is the opening, so you toss paper
scraps / candy wrappers in. Available in 6 colors on MakerWorld (Pink,
Dark Pink, Red, Blue, White, Black). Useful, not just decorative.

## Download

This one's an official MakerWorld upload — easiest path is:

1. Open the MakerWorld link.
2. Tap **Print** → sends straight to Bambu Studio or Bambu Handy with
   the A1 profile already picked.

Or download the `.3mf` directly from the MakerWorld page into this
folder as `kirby-waste-eater.3mf`.

## A1 print settings

Scale **up** for this one — it's meant to be functional.

- Scale: leave at **default** (~80–100 mm tall) so the opening is
  actually useful.
- Layer height 0.20 mm (draft is fine for a desk bin).
- Walls: **4 perimeters** — it gets handled, and you want it to hold
  shape.
- Infill: 5–10 % (hollow enough to be light, enough to not warp).
- Supports: **none** — shape is designed for it.
- Print time: ~2–3 hours at 80 mm.
